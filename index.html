Why Choose Us

We’re a Calais painting company dedicated to providing high-quality painting and carpentry services to central Vermont.

We’ve been serving central Vermont for decades, refining our painting techniques to compliment the unique needs of Vermont. We specialize in painting historic and modern structures with a finish that will stand up to years of Vermont's weather, as well as indoor wear and tear.

We Offer:

  • Expertise to help you choose quality materials
  • A commitment to high standards of workmanship
  • On-time project completion
  • Excellent communication throughout
  • A neat, contained worksite
  • Transparent pricing with detailed estimates
