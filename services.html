Our Painting Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your painting needs

We provide thoughtful prep, repair and painting services for interiors and exteriors. The job starts with a free consultation. If needed, we provide a range of surface preparation, including carpentry.

We warranty our work, as well as the high-quality materials we use, and will return for touch-ups without charge to ensure you are satisfied with your investment.

We’re insured and licensed with the state of Vermont’s Lead-Safe RRPM to do repair and maintenance and inspection of structures that might contain lead, and we’re licensed to work on HUD housing.

Interior Painting

Transform your indoor spaces with expert interior painting. We handle everything from single rooms to entire homes with precision and care.

  • Living rooms and bedrooms
  • Kitchens and bathrooms
  • Offices and commercial spaces
  • Trim and accent walls

Exterior Painting

Painting that looks great and withstands Vermont's challenging weather.

  • House siding and trim
  • Decks and fences
  • Commercial buildings
  • Weatherproofing and sealing

Specialty Services

Beyond standard painting, we offer specialized services to meet unique project requirements.

  • Cabinet refinishing

  • Surface preparation and repair
